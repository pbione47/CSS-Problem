Words of the Week

Bending
Pushing or pulling a string sideways acrodd the fret to raise the pitch of a note.
Fingerstyle
Playing with the fingernails or fingertips with or without finger picks.
Finger Picks
Picks that guitarists use when playing steel-string instruments(lap steel guitar, bass, banjo).
Arpeggio
A broken chord, usually played evenly to a low to high and repeat.
